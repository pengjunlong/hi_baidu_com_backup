--- layout: post title: linux qq报错version number does not start with digit解决方法 time: 2012-03-10 23:34 keywords: 好好学习天天向上 ---

解压linuxqq_v1.0.2-beta1_i386.deb

vim linuxqq_v1.0.2-beta1_i386/DEBIAN/control

去掉版本前“v”，即改为Version: 1.0.2-beta1

重新打包

dpkg-deb -b linuxqq_v1.0.2-beta1_i386  linuxqq.deb

 

 

ok
