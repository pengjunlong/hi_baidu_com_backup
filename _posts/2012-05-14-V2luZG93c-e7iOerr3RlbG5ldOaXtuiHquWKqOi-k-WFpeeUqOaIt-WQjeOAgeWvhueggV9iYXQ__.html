--- layout: post title: Windows终端telnet时自动输入用户名、密码_bat time: 2012-05-14 00:01 keywords: 好好学习天天向上 ---

以登录北邮人论坛为例~~

 

 

@echo off
echo set sh=WScript.CreateObject("WScript.Shell") >telnet_tmp.vbs
echo WScript.Sleep 300 >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo sh.SendKeys "open bbs.byr.cn" >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo WScript.Sleep 300 >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo sh.SendKeys "{ENTER}" >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo WScript.Sleep 300 >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo sh.SendKeys "用户名{ENTER}" >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo WScript.Sleep 300 >>telnet_tmp.vbs
echo sh.SendKeys "密码{ENTER}" >>telnet_tmp.vbs
start telnet
cscript //nologo telnet_tmp.vbs
del telnet_tmp.vbs

 

 

ps: win7默认没有开启telnet功能，需要在控制面板的“程序和功能”中勾上telnet客户端
